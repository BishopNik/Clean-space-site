    Good to know

    Short answers.
    No fine print.

    Do I need to provide cleaning products?

    No. We bring professional products, machines, cloths and protection. We only need access to water and electricity.

    Are your products safe for children and pets?

    Yes. We use biodegradable, carefully dosed formulas and can offer fragrance-free options on request.

    How is the price calculated?

    By area, material, condition and scope. Send photos for a fixed quote before we begin.

    Can you clean outside normal office hours?

    Yes. Early mornings, evenings and selected weekends are available for offices and businesses.

    What if a stain cannot be removed?

    We assess it before treatment and set honest expectations. We never risk damaging a material to chase an impossible result.
